How EXU Detect works

Simple 3-step process from image capture to diagnostic report

Step 1

Capture images

A trained medical assistant takes images of patient's retina using compatible robotic cameras.

Step 2

AI analyzes images

Our dual-layer AI (TensorFlow Lite + Google MedGemma) analyzes images for disease markers in seconds.

Step 3

Diagnostic report

Clear, actionable results: No disease detected (retest in 12 months) or disease detected (refer to specialist).
